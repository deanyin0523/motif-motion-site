Oops, I Married to a Mafia Daddy

Ever since that one-night stand, misfortune has followed me like a curse. A fortune teller predicted that only marriage could break this streak of bad luck! To my surprise, the man I married turned out to be a single father... and a homeless drifter. I thought life after marriage would be quiet and uneventful—but little did I know, my new husband was actually part of the mafia!
